# Signalyz

AI-powered career signal intelligence platform designed to help job seekers understand how employers interpret experience, skills, and positioning against real job requirements.

Live Platform: https://signalyz.ai

---

## Overview

Signalyz analyzes resumes and job descriptions using Claude AI and proprietary scoring systems to generate actionable recommendations without fabricating experience.

Rather than rewriting a user's background, Signalyz helps users communicate existing qualifications more effectively through signal calibration, positioning analysis, and interview intelligence.

The platform was designed, built, and deployed by Demetri Simpson using modern AI tooling, full-stack web technologies, and production cloud infrastructure.

---

## Key Capabilities

### Resume Alignment Engine

Compares candidate resumes against job descriptions and identifies alignment strengths, gaps, and opportunities for stronger positioning.

### Signal Strength Analysis

Evaluates how effectively a candidate communicates experience, skills, impact, and professional positioning.

### Resume Calibration

Generates optimized resume content while preserving factual accuracy and avoiding fabrication.

### Interview Intelligence

Creates interview preparation insights based on job requirements and candidate experience.

### LinkedIn Positioning

Provides recommendations for stronger professional branding and recruiter visibility.

### Cover Letter Generation

Produces tailored cover letters aligned with specific job opportunities.

### Identity Strength Index

Measures candidate positioning across multiple professional dimensions.
